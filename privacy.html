01Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Clickstudio Digital Marketing Co LLC ("we", "us", or the "Operator") handles personal data in connection with the Calco / 01 mobile application (the "App") for iPhone and iPad.

We have written this policy in plain English because most privacy policies are not. Where a regulatory term has a specific meaning, we use it.

This policy applies to the App itself. It does not cover the Apple App Store, which is operated by Apple Inc. and has its own privacy policy.

02What we collect

The App collects and processes the minimum data required to function. Specifically:

  • Purchase receipts issued by Apple at the time of purchase. We use these to confirm that you own the App and to deliver any future updates that you are entitled to. The receipt is handled by Apple's StoreKit framework; we do not see your name, billing details, or Apple ID.
  • Network requests for currency rates. When you use the currency-conversion feature, the App fetches the most recent exchange rates from a public rate-provider endpoint. This request carries the standard information any HTTPS request carries (your device's IP address and a basic user-agent string). It does not carry your name, your Apple ID, or anything that identifies you personally.
  • Crash reports only if you have opted into sharing them with developers in your iOS Privacy & Security settings. These are managed and anonymised by Apple before we receive them.

The App contains no advertising SDK, no analytics SDK, and no third-party tracking of any kind. Your calculation history, settings, and any data you enter stay on your device.

03How we use it

We use the limited data described above to:

  • verify that you have legitimately purchased the App;
  • deliver currency rates so the converter works;
  • diagnose crashes you have explicitly chosen to share;
  • respond to support questions when you write to us.

We do not sell, lease, or trade personal data. We do not use it for advertising. We do not profile you.

04Third parties

Two third parties are involved in normal operation of the App:

  • Apple Inc. operates the App Store and the payment, receipt, update, and crash-reporting infrastructure that the App uses. Apple's handling of your data is governed by Apple's own privacy policy.
  • A public currency-rates provider. When the currency feature is used, the App sends an HTTPS request to a public exchange-rate endpoint. The endpoint sees the request and your IP address (as any web request would), but receives no user identifier from us.

We do not share data with any other party except where required by applicable law or to enforce these Terms.

05Data retention

We operate no servers, databases, or accounts. We retain no personal data on our infrastructure because there is no infrastructure that holds it.

Apple retains purchase receipts under its own policies. The currency-rates provider retains standard HTTP request logs under its own policies. If you email us at the address below, we retain only the contents of your message for as long as needed to answer it, and we delete the thread once the matter is closed.

06Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights under the United Arab Emirates Federal Decree-Law No. 45 of 2021 on the Protection of Personal Data (the "UAE PDPL"), the European Union's General Data Protection Regulation (the "GDPR"), the United Kingdom GDPR, the California Consumer Privacy Act, or other equivalent frameworks.

These rights commonly include:

  • the right to know what personal data is processed about you;
  • the right to request correction of inaccurate data;
  • the right to request deletion of your data;
  • the right to object to or restrict certain processing;
  • the right to data portability;
  • the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

Because we do not run user accounts or store personal data on our infrastructure, most of these rights apply to data held by Apple under Apple's separate policies. To exercise any right against us, please write to info@clickstudio.ae and we will respond within thirty days.

07Children

The App is not directed at children under the age of thirteen. The App Store age rating reflects this. We do not knowingly process the personal data of children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

08International transfers

The Operator is established in the United Arab Emirates. Apple processes App Store and crash-report data through its own international infrastructure. The currency-rates provider may operate from any jurisdiction. By using the App you understand that these transfers happen and consent to them, to the extent required by your local law.

09Security

The App relies on Apple's App Store security, iOS sandboxing, and HTTPS for any network requests. Calculation history and settings live on your device and are protected by iOS itself. Because we do not operate user accounts or servers, there is no central database that could be breached.

10Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes will be announced in the App's release notes. Your continued use of the App after a change indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

11Contact

If you have any question about this Privacy Policy or about how your data is handled, please write to us. We aim to respond within five working days.
